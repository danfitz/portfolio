About Me

Coming from a philosophy undergrad, my greatest skill is the ability—guided by the principles of logic—to unpack, understand, and assess everything around me. When I found coding and web development, I knew I had discovered a place where I could use my hands to finally build the things I had in my mind.

Today, my goal is to become a full-stack developer working to help grow a product (my own or someone else’s) that in some lasting way makes the world a better, more interesting place.
