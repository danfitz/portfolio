About Me

When I got into software development, I knew I had found the perfect place where I could put my critical thinking skills to full use. Coming from a philosophy undergrad, my greatest skill has always been the ability to think logically. Given the logic expected in coding, being a developer makes me feel like the best version of myself.

As a lifelong learner, my current goal is to learn full-stack, working and growing with a company that is building stuff that makes the world a better, more interesting place.
